Finance

Full double-entry bookkeeping with automated reconciliation, multi-currency support, and comprehensive financial reporting tailored for the travel industry.

  • Double-Entry Bookkeeping
    Odoo-inspired full accounting with chart of accounts, journals, and fiscal year management.
  • Multi-Currency & Tax

  • Automated Reconciliation
    Bank statement import with smart matching against bookings and payments.
  • Budget vs Actuals

Rate Engine

Contracting

Industry-leading hotel contracting engine with per-night SPO/BTC calculation, early bird discounts, and a live rate simulator that handles even the most complex pricing structures.

  • SPO & BTC Rate Engine
    Per-night calculation splits stays between Special Promotion Offer and Back-to-Contract rates — the only system that gets this right.
  • Early Bird Discounts
    EBD conditions apply exclusively to BTC nights, with advance booking days and book-by dates.
  • Multi-Season Pricing
    Seasons, room-type supplements, meal-basis supplements, child policies, and special offers all in one contract.
  • Live Rate Simulator
    Simulate any scenario — dates, rooms, occupancy, meal plan — and instantly see the buying and selling breakdown.

Booking Engine

Reservations

Room-level hotel reservation system connected directly to the contracting engine, with real-time buying/selling rate calculation, markup rules, and automated hotel communication.

  • Room-Level Rate Calculation
    Every room gets a full buying breakdown — SPO nights, BTC nights, EBD, supplements — with one-click recalculation.
  • Selling Markup Engine
    Apply markup rules by hotel, destination, or market. See margin and markup amount per room, per booking.
  • Group & Series Bookings
    Multi-room group bookings with shared rooming list, cancellation deadlines, and deadline alerts.
  • Hotel Communication
    Auto-generates hotel rooming list PDF and sends .eml file — ready for one-click forwarding to the hotel.
Transfer Operations

Traffic

End-to-end transfer management from flight data to driver dispatch. Automatically creates transfer jobs when bookings are confirmed, then manages the entire fleet operation.

  • Auto Job Creation
    Arrival and departure transfer jobs are automatically created from booking flight data — zero manual entry.
  • Dispatch Console
    Real-time job board with driver assignment, vehicle allocation, and status tracking.
  • Fleet Management
    Vehicles, vehicle types, drivers with performance tracking, and zone-based pricing.
  • Guest Booking Integration
    Rep assignments, airport meet-and-greet, and partner price overrides all managed in one place.

Partner Portal

B2B Portal

A dedicated portal for tour operators and travel agents to search availability, make bookings at net rates, manage credit limits, and view their revenue reports.

  • Net Rate Search
    Partners search and book at net buying rates — separate from B2C public prices, with their own markup rules.
  • Credit Management
    Set credit limits per partner, track usage, record payments, and flag overdue accounts automatically.
  • Rate Sheets & Markup
    Configure markup rules and rate sheets per partner, hotel, destination, or market.
  • Partner Reports
    Booking summaries, revenue analytics, margin reports, and partner statements with balance tracking.
