# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and professional environment for everyone involved in this project.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Professional and respectful communication
- Constructive feedback and collaboration
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and addressed appropriately.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including the repository, issue tracker, wiki, and any associated communication channels.
